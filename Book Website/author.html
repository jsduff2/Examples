About the Author

 

Jason Duffield

Writing is more of a hobby for Jason than a career. He spends more time reading than writing. Some of his favorite subjects are horror, science fiction and fantasy. He also has a passion for comics. Jason lives at home with his wife and two sons in Illinois.
