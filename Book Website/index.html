Book Proposal

The Parallel
By Jason Duffield

From Different Worlds
Kedrick is a boy from a world not unlike our own.  Skye is a girl from a world very unlike our own.  The fate of reality itself will bring them together.  Fate is also the reason they are so far apart.

Main Characters
This is the story of two main characters told from each of their own separate points of view.  Kedrick is a boy from Earth.  Skye is a girl from a world in another dimension with technology far more advanced than ours.

How Will Our Characters Meet
Kedrick accidentally opens a portal between the dimensions.  In the middle of an intense battle, Skye foils an effort by the enemy to capture him.

An Adventure for the Ages
This is a story of adventure, friendship, loyalty and love.  Aimed at early teens, this story will contain elements of romance and violence.  These elements will be appropriate for the aforementioned age group.
